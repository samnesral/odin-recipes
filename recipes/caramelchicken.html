Vietnamese Caramel Chicken

A delicious sweet and savory delight!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Fish Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Coconut Water
  • Jalepeño
  • Lime

Method

  1. Cut chicken into bite sized pieces
  2. Cook chicken in sugar, fish sauce and coconut water
  3. When tender and saucy, add jalepeño and lime
